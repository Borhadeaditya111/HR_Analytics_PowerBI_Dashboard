# HR_Analytics_PowerBI_Dashboard
This Power BI dashboard is an HR Analytics tool that helps an organization improve employee performance and increase retention. It analyzes attrition across key demographic and job factors such as age, department, salary, and job role providing insights that support data-driven HR decisions and effective retention strategies.

# Key Metrics and KPIs
The dashboard prominently displays crucial Human Resources Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):

Total Employee Count: 1,416

Total Attrition: 229

Attrition Rate: 16.17%

Average Age (Avg_Age): 37 years

Average Salary (Avg_Salary): 6.5K

# Key Analysis and Insights
The dashboard focuses on key areas that influence employee turnover, providing targeted insights:

Attrition by Education Field:

The highest attrition is observed among employees with backgrounds in Life Sciences (38.4%) and Medical (24.9%), indicating an urgent need for targeted retention strategies in these fields.

Attrition by Age Group:

The 26-35 age group accounts for the highest number of attritions (111), highlighting that the company is losing a significant portion of its mid-career/young professional talent.

Attrition by Job Role:

Roles like Laboratory Technician (60) and Sales Executive (55) show the highest attrition counts, suggesting potential issues with workload, career growth, or job satisfaction within these specific teams.

Job Satisfaction:

A breakdown of Job Satisfaction levels (rated 1 to 4) across different job roles helps pinpoint where low satisfaction is most severely contributing to the overall attrition rate.

# Tools and Technologies Used
Power BI Desktop: Utilized for data import, transformation, modeling, and creation of all visualizations.

Power Query (M Language): Used for cleaning and transforming the raw HR dataset.

DAX (Data Analysis Expressions): Applied to create custom calculations and key performance indicators like Attrition Rate, Avg_Age, and Avg_Salary.

Visualization Types: KPI Cards, Donut Chart, Bar Charts, and an Area Chart (Attrition by Year).
